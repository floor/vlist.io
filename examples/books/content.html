Books

Virtualized data table with withTable + withAsync + withScale, backed by a real SQLite database of 40.8 million books from Open Library. Data loads lazily in chunks as you scroll — sorting, filtering, and search all happen server-side via /api/books. withScale + withScrollbar handle the compressed scroll space needed for 40M+ items (far beyond the browser's ~16.7M pixel limit).
